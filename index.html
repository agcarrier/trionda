02 — The object

The ball,
deconstructed

Tri + Onda

Four interlocking panels fold a single wave around the sphere — three nations riding one line.

Debossed grooves

The wave is carved, not printed. Recessed seams steady flight and hold the strike in wet or dry.

Gold flake

Metallic linework threads every panel — a reminder of the only object this ball answers to.

Three icons

A maple leaf, an eagle, a star — one emblem per host, printed at the crest of each wave.
